Tools and Resources

These are the various spaces, tools, and supplies we have. They are available for members to use at the space only. Feel free to contact us about donations of tools and supplies, however we are only accepting tools in working order.

All of our spaces are alcohol free during normal operation and regular business hours.

Classroom, Art Studio, and Event Space

This space can fit several tables and is usable for classes, workshops, and other events. It can also be a quiet place to work on your art when not in use for any of the previous. Members have the ability to hold meetings or socials here once a month, even if some of the attendees aren’t already members, as long as they are open to the public. Just send us an email so we don’t double book our calendar. This space is also bookable for private events; contact us for rates and details.

Events in this space are limited to those 8 and older (accompanied by an adult). Many events set their own age minimum so please check the appropriate event postings.

  • Reconfigurable tables and chairs
  • 44″ display (with HDMI inputs and a Chromecast)
  • Consumables (paint, paper, glue, …)*
  • Easels
  • Miscellaneous art supplies (scissors, etc.)
  • Paint brushes and palettes
  • Sewing machines

* These are included as part of monthly memberships and may also be included as part of class costs. They cost extra for those with a day pass.

Garage Space

The shop includes our pottery area and other work areas. No alcohol is allowed in this space at any time.

  • Generic Linux-based work/editing computer
  • Spray booth
  • 3 Soldering stations
  • Hand tools (screwdrivers, pliers, tape measures, etc.)
  • 2 Kilns
  • Pottery wheel

Electronics Lab

The electronics lab is a partly secluded room attached to the back of the garage. Check our calendar to see when the next 3D printer or laser cutter tool training is scheduled. If there isn’t one available soon send us an email or message us on Facebook to arrange one.

Members must be 13 years or older (accompanied by an adult until 18) to use. The laser cutter operators must be 18 years or older. No alcohol is allowed in this space at any time.

  • Computer with internet access
  • Laser Cutter** (Details)
  • 3x 1.75mm filament 3D printers** (Details)
  • Computer-controlled paper cutter
  • Oscilloscope
  • Soldering iron (variable temperature)
  • Assorted tools (digital caliper, etc.)
  • Miscellaneous parts, cables, and wires

** Training required

Metal and Woodworking Shop

The shop is at the back of our building and includes many useful tools. This is a list of the major tools we have available for use. Many of these require you to take training first before you can use them. Check our calendar to see when the next tool training is scheduled. If there isn’t one available soon send us an email or message us on Facebook to arrange one.

Members must be 18 years or older to use the shop, no exceptions. No alcohol is allowed in this space at any time.

  • Bench grinder
  • Benchtop belt sander
  • Benchtop router table
  • Chop saw
  • Circular saws
  • Corded and cordless drills
  • Drill press
  • Heat guns
  • Metal band saw
  • Metal lathe
  • Metal mill
  • Metal slip roller (hand cranked; adjustable)
  • Reciprocating saw
  • Scroll saw
  • Table saw
  • Welders (MIG wire feed, air stick welder)***

*** Special training separate from the general tool training is required to use these.
