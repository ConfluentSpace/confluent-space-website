Tools and Spaces

These are the various spaces, tools, and supplies we have. They are available for members to use at the space only. Feel free to contact us about donations of tools and supplies, however we are only accepting tools in working order. We are also willing to work out equipment loans if you do not wish to donate. Check out our loan agreement for details on how this works.

All of our spaces are alcohol free during normal operation and regular business hours.

Classroom, Art Studio, and Event Space

This space can fit several tables and is usable for classes, workshops, and other events. It can also be a quiet place to work on your art when not in use for any of the previous. Members have the ability to hold meetings or socials here once a month, even if some of the attendees aren’t already members, as long as they are open to the public. Just send us an email so we don’t double book our calendar. This space is also bookable for private events; contact us for rates and details.

Events in this space are limited to those 8 and older (accompanied by an adult). Many events set their own age minimum so please check the appropriate event postings.

  • Reconfigurable tables and chairs
  • 44″ display (with HDMI inputs and a Chromecast)

Garage Space

The garage includes our various supplies and and other work areas. No alcohol is allowed in this space at any time.

  • Large granite surface table (for working with very flat items)
  • Spray booth
  • 3D printers (see next section)

3D Printer Area

The 3d printer area occupies a corner of our garage and offers access to several different printers of varying sizes. Check our calendar to see when the next 3D printer tool training is scheduled. If there isn’t one available soon send us an email or message us on Facebook to arrange one.

Members must be 13 years or older (accompanied by an adult until 18) to use. No alcohol is allowed in this space at any time.

3D Printer Information

Electronics Lab

The electronics lab is a partly secluded room attached to the back of the garage. Check our calendar to see when the next small CNC or laser cutter tool training is scheduled. If there isn’t one available soon send us an email or message us on Facebook to arrange one.

Members must be 13 years or older (accompanied by an adult until 18) to use. The laser cutter operators must be 18 years or older. No alcohol is allowed in this space at any time.

  • Small CNC (router) machine**
  • Laser Cutter** (Details)
  • Oscilloscope
  • Benchtop power supplies
  • Soldering irons (variable temperature)
  • Assorted tools (digital caliper, etc.)
  • Miscellaneous parts, cables, and wires

** Special training is required to use these.

Backyard

  • Walk-in spray booth
