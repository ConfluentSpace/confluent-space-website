How can I contribute?

We are member and volunteer run, and as such we need many contributions whether they be supplies, time, knowledge, or money. However we have identified a few key areas below.

Teaching

Teachers are our best asset. We are always looking for volunteers to offer a variety of interesting classes to keep people coming and help our mission of education. We have offered classes from mail art and sewing to Python and 3D printing.

If you are interested in teaching or know someone who is, please email us at confluent.space@gmail.com or stop by the space during business hours. Prospective teacher should read our informational page on scheduling a class.
