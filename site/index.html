What is Confluent?

Confluent is a 501(c)3 non-profit with space and tools to bring your imagination into reality.

Learn More

What do we do?

We host classes, workshops, and events as well as provide a space for people to learn, work on their projects, and have fun.

See What's Hot

How can I contribute?

We accept various monetary and non-monetary donations or support. Every dollar donated is invested directly into the space or its events.
